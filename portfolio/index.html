E-commerce Website Redesign

I led the redesign of a major e-commerce website, focusing on enhancing the user journey from browsing to checkout. Through extensive user research and testing, I identified pain points and streamlined the navigation, resulting in a 20% increase in conversion rates. The new design featured a modern, clean interface with intuitive product categorization and an optimized mobile experience, ensuring a seamless shopping experience across all devices.

Mobile Banking App

Designed a mobile banking app aimed at providing users with easy access to their financial information while ensuring top-notch security. By prioritizing user feedback and conducting usability tests, I created an intuitive and visually appealing interface. Key features included quick access to account balances, transaction histories, and a streamlined process for money transfers and bill payments. The app's user-friendly design significantly improved customer satisfaction and engagement.

Fitness Tracker Dashboard

Developed an interactive dashboard for a fitness tracker, integrating data visualization techniques to present users with insights into their health and fitness progress. The project involved creating detailed wireframes and prototypes, followed by user testing to refine the interface. The final design offered users a comprehensive view of their activity levels, workout trends, and health metrics, all presented in an easy-to-understand format. This project helped users stay motivated and achieve their fitness goals through a visually compelling and user-friendly interface.
